Sunlands.com, one of the largest Self-Taught Higher Education Examinations and Vocational Education institutions in China, is committed to improving the competitiveness of users in their workplace and has trained more than 1 million students.

Sunlands.com has a wide range of training courses and services, ranging from vocational qualification certification to skills training. According to the Mobile Application Shangdejigou APP on IOS and Android, Sunlands.com is developing at a fast speed and listed on NASDAQ last year.

Product Manager

Work Experience

After graduation from college in 2016 summer, I joined this online education company to develop my interest in the mobile application and user interaction further.

From the beginning, my work was about user research based on some qualitative methods like interviewing and focused group combined with quantitative data analysis from the online survey. According to these, I was assigned to the group focused on the forum and message functions to provide communication service for our users. It would be very helpful for the users to be persistent during their learning since the characteristic for users is impulse and give up so easily with lower education level but wanted to seek an opportunity for career development.

So I designed new forum entrance and message list for them to help the users know their college forum and program sub-forum and could talk with their teachers, mentors and classmates right after registration. And by cleaning the old structure and data of the forum, the user’s weekly interaction data increased dramatically.

For the product management works, I closely collaborate with the design, engineering, marketing, business development and teaching team for needs and requirements collection, product requirements evaluation, developing and testing.

Sunlands.com Mobile Forum

Project 1: Forum DAU and MAU Improvement

Product Design Goals

1. Helping the users to be persistent in learning and self-study
2. Increasing the selling of our products and the NPS indicator
3. Decreasing the return on purchase or order

KPI 1

Improving the user activity and interaction measure by DAU and MAU in sunlands.com forum TAB

Problem

After moving the forum from the old version to the new native building version forum, the user activity declined drastically

Field Research



Location: The registration offices around Beijing City and Tianjin City area
Timeline: 2016.9.30-10.7
Purpose: To find needs from the registration offices staffs and potential
customers who came to the office and consulted.
Content: Chatting and asking some questions about the experience of using the mobile application and studying by themselves.
Observation: There is a pain point of finding their classmates and contacting their mentor on the mobile application.



Research from an online survey- Main user feedbacks



Needs Collection and Redefine







Ideas and Needs from Decision Makers, like CEO, CMO and CTO
From the strategic perspective, they wanted to show a clear structure of our business and a lot of majors and programs in our company to the free users who will be channelled into our mobile app by marketing and SEO.







Teachers and Mentors
They complained a lot during our weekly mingle meeting that the main mission for them now is to encourage their students to persist their studying rather than really help them to study correctly.







Self-study Students
They have no sense of belonging of their program and college. It’s hard for them to find the collect channel to post their question or interaction with classmates or teachers.

Problem Definition 1


1. Low user activity brought by
1)Students don't know who are their classmates and who are their teachers
2)Students don't know which sub-forum they could post to get better and more usful feedback

Personna Update

Lower the learning cost Reason: User Persona Lower mobile using habits or capacity Cheaper and lower stability mobile operating system

Solution and Direction

How to:

2. Aim at teachers who don’t have an account: Use their mobile phone number in our human resource system with the phone number in the forum system
