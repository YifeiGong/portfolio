Nip

A Mixed-Reality Projection Interactive Device for Vehicle Rear Entertainment

PROJECT TYPE Summer School Conceptual Project
DURATION 3 Days in July 2015
Instructors Xiaohua Sun, Wei Liu

Overview

Nip-nip is a rear seat entertainment which could randomly provide real-time interaction and multi-media information about various sights along the road for the user by the AR on the rear window and the nipping behavior from the point on the AR to the in-car environment.

Design Condition: Preliminary Design Plan
