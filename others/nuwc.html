My Design is a feature embedded in Northwestern University APP Wildcat Connection. In order to make student more engage in students organization affairs, I designed the searching feature more visible, accessible and reachable for new Northwestern students.

It could help them to find an organization even if just glanced at the poster on second. This feature is a tech-driven feature by scanning, recoginizing and matching poster picture to find an organization or an event.

Design Challenge Topic

Design an experience for new students to browse, search, and propose new student organizations.

Design Requirments






1. The motivation for finding an organization for new students is declining with time and the amount of workload of their courses. So the motivation to join or propose an organization is precious and by using design they could go through this process easier and join their interested organization quicker.






2. Why focus on mobile end? Sometimes the privacy for joining an organization is important. And based on one big touch point of being reached out by posters, the context of crowded place and the sequence behavior of taking pictures, this interaction quality should be swift and efficient.




Design Direction

1. Building a feature in the NU APP_Wildcats Connection Function

1. Since student organization affairs is a big touch point for new students starting using the NU APP, so the university could also by this way to attract more students download this APP and make the other essential functions reaching out students, like NU help and safe ride.

2. The organization databased is existed so this mobile new version only need to build a front-end UX and UI.
