INTRO

PERMA compost barrel is designed for indoor compost and planting amateur. It helps the user to make fertilizer more accessible and do not bring odor, insect even mouse in the house. Based on the permaculture standard, the recycle plastic bottle lifecycle can be extended by storing the compost liquid.

Candidate works of Milan Expo Chinese student design works exhibition




Field Study and Research

For the most of people in Beijing, the first image of Daxing District is watermelon which is one of the most important fruits in Beijing’s summer. Daxing is a big agriculture area near the center area in Beijing. And the farmers also provide strawberry, various vegetables, milk and beer for the citizen’s daily consuming.

More than 4,000 villagers in 17 villages in Yinghai Town moved from their original land to the seven Nanhai Resettlement Communities. Most of them were professional farmer previously.

[Before resettling]
The residents planted vegetables on individual plots the self-field for private eating.



[After resettling]
Now they live in the big comfortable apartments with the public garden in the community.



The apartments all face south, and the public garden is located in the center of the community. The building span is appropriate, and the virescence is good.

Reflections and Future

In this project, the pain points were not provided by the users but were dug by myself. That changed my cognition of user research and interview. Questionaire might be a good tool for testing pain points rather than a tool to collect the points since the user's ideas may lead the designers to a dilemma.

The manufacture feasibility should be tested before producing. And the package design could use the recycled material which will be consistent with the design theme.
