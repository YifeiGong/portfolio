 This project is a service design for a chain dental clinic to help them improve their dental service and enhance the online reservation platform efficiency. A mini program based on Wechat platform, a renewed VI system are the outputs of this project. According to these, the users could save a lot of time to make a reservation and solve their dental problem.




Project Background

Project Source
Product System Design Coursework(Year 2)

Design Theme
Private Dental Clinic

Design Target
KEWA Chain Dental Clinic in Beijing

Field Study and Research



KEWA Dental is a chain of dental companies specializing in mid-high-end dental services, usually located in the office floor or affiliated business district. The choice of KEWA Dental as our service design object is because KEWA Dental is oriented to high-end consumers, and the enterprise itself has a good corporate image concept, service concept, and the intention to improve, strength and space.

Research Results






2. User Definition

According to customer and user definition, we could provide differentiated services for various types of users.

Guidelines:
1. Defining customer value and classifying them from different perspectives, such as consumer behavior, desktop, and mobile internet acceptance

2. Carrying on the analysis according to the various consumer behavior and consumer psychology to

3. Providing different service based on different consumer behavior modes

The elderly





Corresponding Service Design

1. Establishing a comprehensive and detailed paper personal file and creating easy access for the elderly to check

2. Providing free shuttle service in the city; design treatment chair consisting of the elderly ergonomics

3. Creating a brochure to record the dental service of the elderly each time, and the time of the next appointment

4. Providing personalized service and allocating specialist for each customer to build trust

Adults





Corresponding Service Design

1. Establishing a mobile app to provide registration service including checking the latest schedule for each physician, the price, duration, and evaluation of each physician and each program, making an appointment or rescheduling in the app, etc.

2. Focusing the humanization service and playing the movie or music that the client likes in the process of diagnosis and treatment, etc.

3. Establishing a comprehensive and detailed personal electronic profile, reminder platform for the full service

Kids





Corresponding Service Design

1. Setting up a children's room with a lively and lovely style of the diagnosis room to decrease terror emotion

2. Playing their favorite animations for children to attract their attention; design seats that can guide children not to move

Guideline:

Users proportion + Clinic type= Target user

Target Users: middle and young white-collars with higher consumption capacity

The user Needs: Solving dental problems quickly and efficiently


The current customer journey map

After field investigation, the customer journey map was used to find out where the difficulties and users' expectations to find the breakthrough of our solution.

We used stickers to write out what everyone did, thought, felt, heard and touched in the visiting session.

Taking KEWA Dental as our service design target, we first determined the customer and the primary user and then implied various analysis methods to analyze the customer behavior and service flow separately to systemize and visualize the user experience flow. Moreover, we concerned about the users and business touch points and pain points.

Demands systemization and Missions

Mission 1:
Modifying the current customer journey map to improve existing insufficient touch points, find possible new touch points, or redundant some.

Mission 2:
Listing all pain points and select no less than three to resolve in the next step.




The renewed service blueprint

Goal:
To build a new service system and user experience process, and visualize them.


We described the service flow, the service scenario, some service providers, customer persona and user experience process; decomposed the relationship between the front-end and the back-end and the various activities and rearranged their order; combined with stakeholder map to recognize the whole service relationship.






Realization: mobile-end reservation system design



Basis: The existing Web online reservation system
Mission: Building an online reservation platform on mobile-end that fits the using scene





Interaction Design: Mini Program on Wechat

The development of mobile medicine is restricted by the medical system of China, but the stomatology industry is the earliest commercialized marketization in our country. Each year, the market faces 190+ million teeth that need treatment, and the competition for mobile dental medicine is a mirror in the field of mobile medicine, a microcosm. Because dental characteristics determine that no matter how mobile interconnection, communication between doctors and patients, the final treatment must be offline, this is a standard O2O business model that unlike many medical disciplines that can deal with some problems remotely or online.
