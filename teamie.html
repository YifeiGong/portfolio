Overview

My role

I integrated the resources in MEM 490 and CS 497 to develop this product.

1. Researched pain points during the pre and post team-building process
2. Designed a web product that supports polling, planning, scheduling and collecting feedback
3. Managed roadmap and development progress in two teams and served as the R&D Lead.

As a former product manager, I was inspried from the team building prganization process when I was doing my summer internship at Microsoft. I pitched and built this idea in MEM 490 Product Management and CS 497 Rapid Prototyping classes as team leader.

This project has been accepted by the Garage as Tinker Program to incubate.

Final Pitch

Pitch 1.0

For team leaders, they are facing a constant challenge that they need to arrange a team-building event regularly. Weekly or monthly, they feel tired to accommodate team member's preferences, time, and limitations to schedule a team event to make their team more cohesive.

Teamie is a web-based product to help your team make an agreement on your next time team building more efficiently, by creating a venue poll based on event type, date, and an area set by the organizer, sending out page for voting, collecting results and making reservation, gathering event feedback, setting regular notifications to make team building overall experience better with less efforts.
