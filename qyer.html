Qyer.com

QYER, founded in Germany in 2004 and now headquartered in Beijing, was originally a travel community of overseas Chinese sharing their experiences and tips on travelling around Europe. The 12-year-old company is sticking to its core business of better outbound travel amid the ups and downs of the tech industry. For an average Chinese person, preparing for outbound trips can be a daunting task.

They are faced with issues such as visas, language, and even differing climates. This is how QYER, “China’s largest one-stop outbound travel platform”, is making a difference by making these tasks much easier for its users.

Asspciate Product Manager

Inspired by the book called user experience elements, I attracted by product manager’s career and started to explore some opportunities of PM intern for my junior year. After several rounds interview, I got this PM internship at QYER.com which is the biggest Independent Travel Experience Sharing Forum that was exploring their commercialization path. I joined the Commercial Product Department and under the product manager’s instruction who directly under the CEO.

For the starting point, I conducted the daily selling data analysis to share with the product manager and marketing colleagues to help them adjust their work and maintain or increase the selling data or response to the global festivals or some special themes, like dramas, movies or social events.

After two week’s learning the basic mindsets and skillsets of product management and building up the relationship with the design and developing team, I started to get involved in the PM work. I designed some new function and iterated some old function based on users feedback, data we collected like clicking or buying, and some idea from the management and strategic level.

For the product design part, I designed the new visa and cruise channel in the app since the business development colleagues wanted to make some promotions and to put some instructions for the users who have interests in buying visa and cruise products but need more information about the whole process and experience.

For the other supporting issues, I was assigned to provide some features for our business partners like to exchange user traffic with other mobile application in the homepage carousel figures. Thus I also enhanced my awareness and soft skills of marketing and operating with other departments, like designing the sharing coupon function to other social media. I also wrote the scripts for everyday product promotion pushing to their devices.
